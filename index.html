ABOUT

Team Endeavour is the official robotics team of SLIET Longowal, founded by quintuplets from various engineering backgrounds in 2014. The delegation provides a wholesome technical milieu where the inquisitive minds get a platform to sculpt their knacks that transfrom into greater assets that indeed land them in handsome pursuits. At present the team comprises of 50+ smartworking rookies, leading by example, instigating comon interest in Robotics, from diverse fields work in harmony marshaling their departments from the upfront. Every individual in the team is well versed with programming, circuit-designing, fabricating and simulation, the skills that are prerequisites in this realm. With every passing year the team plans to push it's limits and come out of the comfort zone.

VISION

The team was founded with the intentions to encourage engineering undergraduates to be able to understand different technology stacks used in robotics, outside their regular curriculum. Now, the team participates in competitions both at college as well as national levels. The main motive is to make students aware of the upcoming technologies not just by mugging up the theories but also by practically implementing the theories they've learnt. The outstanding achievements of the team in the last few years has attracted a great amount of eligible students to its door, thereby helping the team excel in every field,either be learning or managing and holding the team together. Hence, with ambitious goals and a well built team, the team has decided to take part in international events too.

Our Work

Quadcopters

Hold on your breath and get ready for the future of e-sports! Yes, Drones are the future of sports. Intricately designed, lightning speed, enthralling thrust, gorgeous carbon-fibre sleek aerodynamic figure flying in the sky is a dream for every DRL pilot and here you have this opportunity to feed your inner sky rider. Make yourself ready to “Build , crash, repair and fly again and rule the sky all over.”

RC Car

Love the sound of a roaring engine? The smell of burning fuel? Then RC Car is here for you. An RC Car is a nitro-engine car that accelerates within seconds to breathtaking speeds that is tough to follow. The fuel used is nitromethyle, that appetizes the hunger of a FormulaOne. This raging hobby is for the speed demons seeking some serious kickstart. Enthusiastic rookies can churn their driving skills, make jumps & turns, and catch some air with full throttle to unleash their fangs.

Line Followers

Welcome to this new era of technology where machines use their wit & intelligence to take decisions, and carry out their daily tasks. LFR is no exception which is a miniature model of it. An LFR (Line Following Robot) follows a particular path using various sensors whereas a maze solver traverses through complex mazes. A real-life example, that is implemented in warehouses of large tech giants like Amazon, AliBaba, etc. This helps students brush up their logic building using complex path planning algorithms.

Rover

Space Exploration!!! This word ignites our thoughts to think beyond our boundaries, to know the unknown, to explore the hidden secrets of our mysterious space, where humans can’t put their footsteps whereas a Rover can. A rover is autonomous bot which is used for space exploration. It is the massive challenge for every engineer, for every mad space lover, to ignite there inner STARWARS-REBEL and build a space exploration rover using their ultimate skills and ideas.

RC Plane

Nowdays, aeromodeling is one of the most exciting and enjoyable hobbies in existence and making your own RC Plane is like a dream come true for all those young rookies who always dream to fly and conquer the sky. An RC plane inheriting all the dynamics of an actual aircraft, gives you the real excitement of an "AIR FORCE ONE" pilot while flying it. From gliding smoothly in the air to reaching raging velocities, it uses the perfect balance of the four forces lift, drag, weight and thrust to fly like an eagle in the sky.

BattleBots

Pumped up with great vigor & adrenaline? Want to release your anger and destroy things out? Then buddy, you are at the perfect place! BattleBots are the muscular & heavy death vader bots which are here to demolish everything that comes in front. Ranging from few kilos to almost quintals, these are real metal hungry bots. Bots are equipped with variety of weapons ranging from bulky spinners to deadly cutters. Witness sparks fly as metal crushes metal in this magnificent battel of metal warriors.
