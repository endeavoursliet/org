ABOUT

Team Endeavour is the official robotics team of SLIET Longowal, founded by quintuplets from various engineering backgrounds in 2014. The delegation provides a wholesome technical milieu where the inquisitive minds get a platform to sculpt their knacks that transfrom into greater assets that indeed land them in handsome pursuits. At present the team comprises of 50+ smartworking rookies, leading by example, instigating comon interest in Robotics, from diverse fields work in harmony marshaling their departments from the upfront. Every individual in the team is well versed with programming, circuit-designing, fabricating and simulation, the skills that are prerequisites in this realm. With every passing year the team plans to push it's limits and come out of the comfort zone.

VISION

The team was founded with the intentions to encourage engineering undergraduates to be able to understand different technology stacks used in robotics, outside their regular curriculum. Now, the team participates in competitions both at college as well as national levels. The main motive is to make students aware of the upcoming technologies not just by mugging up the theories but also by practically implementing the theories they've learnt. The outstanding achievements of the team in the last few years has attracted a great amount of eligible students to its door, thereby helping the team excel in every field,either be learning or managing and holding the team together. Hence, with ambitious goals and a well built team, the team has decided to take part in international events too.

MISSION

The team was initially started to reinforce the technical prospect of students, enabling them to become refined concocts having knowledge of diverse fields. Following the current trends, everyone is in hunt to become that polymath, who is capable of handling any work assigned. So for this, what will be better than working in the field of robotics. A field that needs no introduction where people from varied backgrounds come and work in harmony, contribute their part and learn in reciprocation. Robotics is the collective implementation of latest technologies & using it for our ease that certainly requires profound technical expertise. According to a survey conducted by a reputed firm, around 80% of engineers are not employable for the industry. These statistics don't need any explanation as such, so our initiative is just a contribution to bring a change & enhance the technical calibre of engineering graduates.

Our Work

AYN

Most of you might be aware of the braille script, right? It is basically a special type of script written in embossed form for the vision-impaired. They feel it by touch, but this script is limited to very few places like the bank notes, or some books, magazines and that's all. What about the places of daily regime? "AYN" - it's an aid for just same. Our device basically uses image proceesing techniques to recognize various texts, labels, logos, pictures, etc and give voice-overs for the same to ease out the process.

APM

It feels gross while standing in long queues, & eagerly waiting for the turn to come for printing in crowd or at any other emergency of printing especially getting your job at night time when there's no printing kiosk around to fulfil your needs. So this device, is a solution to these problems. Our device will provide one stop solution like the current ATMs where one can reach out at any time and get your printing job done without any human involvement.

Automation in Agriculture

Heard of Swarm Robotics? Well, if you have, then have you imagined using it for farming? That's pretty in a nutshell what we are implementing in our Automation in Agriculture. For those going clueless, we are using multiple tiny robots (Swarms) which will execute various tasks of irrigation such as ploughing, planting, cutting, etc. These swarms will be connected to a single IOT ecosystem that will fetch data from various sensors which monitor temperature, humidity,etc & send data to the server automating the process of irrigation.

Guardian

ICU(s) may be the most dreaded places a person can imagine. How about being admitted to the same and being in great need of help but tough to find any? That's the situation for which we are working for! At present, there's a helper to call out for in case of an emergency. But what if he/she fails to be present at the right time & spot? Our device ensures the exact addressal of the need to the concerned at the right moment by capturing the facial expression of the patient and using it for sending specific messages.

Smart WheelChair

Currently, patients have a tough time manouevering the wheelchair that involves using their hands to drive. Also, vision-impaired require helpers for their supervision. In order to remove this dependency, our smartwheelchair comes with joystick feature for smooth walkaround coupled with voice-controlled support for the blind. But this is not all, the wheelchair is also equipped with modern smart IOT system that connects to the app and helps to navigate in a predefined path, & moreover keep the track using GPS.

ECPCB

Electronically Controlled & Programmed Circuit (ECPCB) is modern smart alternative to the contemporary MCBs used in our houses which trips down at high voltage levels.

Portable OCR

Well, most of us must have used many Optical Character Recognisers, CamScanner being the most widely used in smartphones. How about making a dedicated device that can perform many tedious tasks and automate the current work culture? Our device performs just the same. Various modes in which our device can perform are the meter reader, text reader, number plate detection and the label extraction mode. These can help in automating the current manual work culture and also speed up the current work process.

RC Car

Love the sound of a roaring engine? The smell of burning fuel? Then RC Car is here for you. Line Followers

Welcome to this new era of technology where machines use their wit & intelligence to take decisions, and carry out their daily tasks. LFR is no exception which is a miniature model of it. An LFR (Line Following Robot) follows a particular path using various sensors whereas a maze solver traverses through complex mazes. A real-life example, that is implemented in warehouses of large tech giants like Amazon, AliBaba, etc. This helps students brush up their logic building using complex path planning algorithms.
